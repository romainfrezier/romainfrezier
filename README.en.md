🚀 I'm 24 years old and a Computer Science and Management engineer, graduated from [Polytech Montpellier](https://www.polytech.umontpellier.fr/), France.

<img src="img/logo-k.svg" style="width: 10px; margin-right: 3px; margin-left: 2px;" alt="Infomaniak Logo"/> I currently work at <a href="https://www.infomaniak.com/en">Infomaniak | The Ethical Cloud</a>, in Geneva, as a Developer Team Leader in the Events & Marketing team, where I manage a team of 12 developers.
